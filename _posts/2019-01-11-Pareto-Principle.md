### Pareto Principle

The Pareto principle states that 80% of the outcomes result from just 20% of the effort. 

Also known as the 80/20 rules or the law of the vital few, and a way to manage the time better and get more done with less effort. 

This rules is important to understand because it helps to prioritize what to do first. Prioritize our tasks over some of the other things on that to do list, event side hustles which generate less money.
