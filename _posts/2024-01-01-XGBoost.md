### Summary


XGBoost (Extrem Gradient Boosting) is a powerfull algorithm that has become the most popular tools for structured data prediction tasks. This algorithm combine boosting with advanced regularization, tree pruning, and parallelization techniques. Also it has high performance in classification, regression, and ranking problems. 


### Inputs

  - Numerical
  - Categorical

### Preprocessing

  - Data needs to be in a D-Matrix
  



### Advantages
  - High speed performances and scalability combined with pruning make it a go-to in machine learning.
  - Flexible both types structured and unstructured data.
  - Flexible that allows to be applied to a wide range of tasks such as regression, classification, ranking, and more.
